Questions

Is Brand Growth Studio the right fit for you?

We’re a good fit if you care about strategy and performance as much as you care about aesthetics. Here are a few common questions.

What types of businesses do you work with?

We primarily support service-based businesses, B2B companies and online brands that rely on their website to generate leads or sales.

How much does a project cost?

Pricing depends on scope, but most website projects fall into a clear range after a short discovery call.

You’ll always receive a written scope and quote before we begin, so you know exactly what’s included.

Do you offer ongoing marketing support?

Yes. For some clients we simply redesign the site; for others we also help with landing pages, paid ads and ongoing optimization.

We can start with a one-off project and grow into a retainer if it makes sense for your business.

Will I be able to update the site myself?

We typically build on user-friendly platforms and provide a simple handover so your team can edit copy and images without touching code.
